Overview

Our mental health application is designed to help you track your mood, journal your thoughts, and stay on top of your mental well-being. Whether you're looking to improve your mental health, manage stress, or simply reflect on your day, our app provides the tools you need to take control of your mind and emotions.

Features & Benefits

Mood Tracker

Track your mood throughout the day and identify patterns in your emotions.

Journaling

Maintain a daily journal to reflect on your thoughts, experiences, and progress.

Self-Care Reminders

Set reminders for self-care activities to ensure you're taking care of yourself.

Personalized Insights

Receive personalized insights and tips based on your mood and journal entries.
